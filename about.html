Engineering for resilience

SRE and Cloud Infrastructure Conference 2021

The annual conference offers site reliability engineers a forum to discuss important issues around building complex distributed systems. Topics focus on resilience, reliability, and performance and often include automation, testing, security, and organizational culture. The event includes training sessions, workshops, and keynotes on how to create scalable and highly reliable software systems.
